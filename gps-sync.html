🔧 Car Factory — Bookmarklet Setup

Drag either button below to your bookmarks bar for a one-click install.

🛰️ GPS Sync

Registers new finance deals in Passtime automatically.

Step 1 — Install

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

⚡ GPS Sync

Bookmarks bar not visible? Press Ctrl+Shift+B

Step 2 — Each Morning
  1. Go to secure.passtimeusa.com and log in
  2. Complete 2FA on your phone
  3. Once on the dashboard, click ⚡ GPS Sync in your bookmarks
  4. It runs automatically — registers each deal and marks it done
What it does
  • Finds all finance deals with gps_uploaded = false
  • Searches each GPS serial in Passtime
  • Found → updates the record with customer info
  • Not found → creates a new Encore record
  • Marks each deal as done in the app

💳 CarPay Sync

Syncs all customer data and payment history from CarPay into the app.

Step 1 — Install

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

💳 CarPay Sync

Only needs to be installed once.

Step 2 — Run the Sync
  1. Go to dealers.carpay.com and log in
  2. Wait for the customer table to fully load
  3. Click 💳 CarPay Sync in your bookmarks
  4. A panel appears — select DeBary or DeLand
  5. Hit Sync and wait for it to finish (~1–2 min)
  6. Repeat for the other location
What it syncs
  • All active customers — name, account #, days late, next payment
  • Contact info — phone number and email
  • Vehicle details and current balance owed
  • Scheduled payment amount and frequency
  • Full payment history — every payment date, amount, and method
When to run it
  • Before opening the Repo Brain — so it has fresh data
  • After a week of payments to update balances
  • Anytime you want the Late tab current
