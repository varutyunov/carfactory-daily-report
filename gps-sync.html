🛰️ GPS Sync Setup

One-time setup to add the GPS sync bookmarklet to your browser.

Step 1

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

⚡ GPS Sync

If you don't see your bookmarks bar, press Ctrl+Shift+B

Step 2 — Every Morning
  1. Go to secure.passtimeusa.com and log in
  2. Complete 2FA on your phone
  3. Once you're on the dashboard, click ⚡ GPS Sync in your bookmarks
  4. Watch it work — it'll register each GPS and mark them done
What it does
  • Pulls all finance deals with gps_uploaded = false
  • Searches each GPS serial in Passtime
  • If found → edits the record with customer info
  • If not found → adds a new record via Encore
  • Marks each as done in the app
